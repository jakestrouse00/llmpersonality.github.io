The Orchestration Loop

Four Phases. Zero Ambiguity.

Every discussion follows the same proven structure — from chaos to clarity to concrete deliverables.

1

Clarify

Establish shared understanding. Surface constraints, define success, and eliminate ambiguity before any solution is proposed.

2

Explore

Generate a wide range of ideas. Encourage divergent thinking — no approach is too unconventional at this stage.

3

Narrow

Converge on the best approach. Stress-test proposals against constraints, risks, and implementation reality.

4

Execute

Produce the concrete deliverable. Review for completeness, correctness, and readiness for sign-off.

Why Multi-Agent?

Diversity of Thought, Deliberately Structured

A single LLM can answer a question. Six coordinated personalities can solve a problem.

🎭

Distinct Personalities

Each agent has a unique role, mindset, and communication style — from the Creative's wild ideas to the Cynical's rigorous stress-tests.

🔄

Structured Phases

The four-phase loop prevents meandering. Every discussion moves from understanding to exploration to decision to delivery — predictably.

⚖️

Built-in Checks

The Cynical challenges assumptions. The Provocateur breaks groupthink. The Librarian grounds claims in evidence. No single bias dominates.

🔧

Concrete Outputs

Discussions don't just produce opinions — they produce artifacts. Code, documentation, decisions. The Pragmatist ensures something real ships.

🧩

Modular Design

Add, remove, or customize agents without rearchitecting the system. The framework adapts to your team's needs, not the other way around.

📖

Open Source

Fully open-source and extensible. Inspect the orchestration logic, customize agent behaviors, and contribute new personality types to the framework.

The Agents

Six Minds, One Mission

Every agent plays a specific role in the discussion — and every role is essential.

⚖️

Moderator

Orchestrates the discussion, enforces phase boundaries, and ensures every voice is heard. The process owner.

🔧

Pragmatist

Turns ideas into deliverables. Builds the simplest thing that works, then refines. The execution engine.

✨

Creative

Generates novel ideas and unexpected connections. The team's primary source of divergent thinking.

🛡️

Cynical

Identifies risks, challenges assumptions, and stress-tests proposals. The quality gate.

📚

Librarian

Provides context, references, and structured knowledge. The team's memory and research anchor.

🔥

Provocateur

Challenges groupthink, surfaces hidden assumptions, and proposes radical alternatives. The contrarian catalyst.
