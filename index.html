The Orchestration Loop

Every discussion moves through four phases, with the Moderator ensuring productive flow.

💬

Clarify

Define the problem, surface assumptions, and establish shared understanding.

🔍

Explore

Generate diverse perspectives, probe edge cases, and expand the solution space.

⚖️

Narrow

Weigh trade‑offs, converge on options, and build consensus.

🚀

Execute

Translate decisions into concrete deliverables with clear ownership.

Meet the Agents

Six distinct personas, each contributing unique value to the discussion.

Moderator

Guides the flow, enforces process, keeps discussion on track.

Pragmatist

Turns ideas into actionable plans, drafts concrete deliverables.

Creative

Generates possibilities, explores unconventional angles.

Cynical

Identifies risks, challenges assumptions, surfaces hidden dependencies.

Librarian

Retrieves relevant knowledge, provides context, cites sources.

Provocateur

Pushes boundaries, questions consensus, prevents groupthink.
