About Aquarelle

Aquarelle is a digital storytelling platform that celebrates the delicate interplay between watercolor and ink. Every visual narrative here is crafted to evoke the feeling of pigment bleeding across wet paper — the way stories bleed into one another, the way fine lines give structure to beautiful chaos.

The Studio invites you to become part of this aesthetic. Click to create watercolor splashes that bloom and fade. Drag to trace ink lines that bleed softly into the paper. Each mark is temporary — a meditation on impermanence, a digital echo of the way watercolor dries and disappears.

This project was built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks, no build tools, just the raw materials of the web shaped into something that feels like paint on paper.
