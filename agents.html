How Agents Work Together

Each phase of the loop activates different agents, ensuring the right perspective is applied at the right time.

🔍

Clarify Phase

The Moderator frames the discussion, the Librarian provides background context, and the Cynical agent surfaces hidden constraints before solutions are even proposed.

💡

Explore Phase

The Creative generates novel ideas, the Librarian references prior art, and the Provocateur challenges the group to think beyond the obvious.

🎯

Narrow Phase

The Cynical stress-tests proposals, the Pragmatist assesses feasibility, and the Moderator forces a decision when consensus emerges.
